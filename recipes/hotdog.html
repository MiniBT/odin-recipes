Hot Dog

Description

This recipe is for your basic hot dogs, but prepared in an air fryer

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees F
  2. Place buns in the basket of the air fryer and cook 2 minutes. Remove buns to a plate.
  3. Place hot dogs in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 3 minutes. Transfer hot dogs to buns.
